Welcome to Diva Villa

A quiet courtyard inn for travellers between flights.

Tucked into a leafy lane in Kimbulapitiya, Diva Villa was built for the rhythm of long-haul travel — arrivals at three in the morning, early-bird tuk-tuk rides to the airport, families landing weary and hungry. Our courtyard is lantern-lit, our rooms are cool and quiet, and there is always rice on the buffet.

We host guests from over forty countries each month. They come for the proximity to BIA; they return for the warmth.

The Restaurant

A buffet that
tastes like home —
even if home is
nine time-zones away.

Our open kitchen serves a rotating Sri Lankan buffet for breakfast and dinner, with fresh local dishes prepared according to the day’s service. Guests can also order from the menu, making dining easy for solo travellers, families and groups. No fixed prices are published online; current meal options are confirmed directly with the kitchen. Non-resident guests are welcome with advance reservation.

Breakfast 6 – 10 am

Early enough for the 7 am flight — choose Option A or Option B when you book.

Sri Lankan Cuisine

Coconut sambol, hoppers, curries and fresh fruit — cooked fresh each morning.

Local Sourcing

Vegetables and fish from the Negombo market each morning; spice from a family supplier.

Group & Private Dining

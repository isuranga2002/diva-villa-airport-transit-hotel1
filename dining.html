The Restaurant

Breakfast & Dining
at Diva Villa.

Fresh Sri Lankan buffet dining for breakfast and dinner, plus menu ordering for guests who prefer a lighter or more personal meal. No fixed prices are published online.

Your Morning Choice

Breakfast options,
made fresh.

Every guest chooses their morning style — a hearty English Breakfast or a traditional Sri Lankan Breakfast. The new requested items are included below, and all meals are confirmed directly without fixed online prices.

Option A

English Breakfast

A full, satisfying start to your day before a flight or a long journey across the island. Classic ingredients, freshly prepared.

  • Fried or scrambled eggs
  • Toast with butter & jam
  • Baked beans
  • Grilled tomato & sausages
  • Fresh fruit
  • Coffee or Ceylon tea
  • Sausage / fruit plate / butter / jam
Option B

Sri Lankan Breakfast

Experience the real flavours of Sri Lanka on your first morning. A traditional spread that our guests come back for every time.

  • Egg hoppers & string hoppers
  • Milk rice — Kiribath
  • Coconut sambol
  • Dhal curry & fish curry
  • Fresh fruit plate
  • Ceylon tea & coffee
  • fruit plate
Menu Options

Order what suits
your table.

Our open kitchen serves a rotating Sri Lankan buffet for breakfast and dinner. Guests can also order from the menu, so meals can be arranged neatly for solo travellers, families and groups without publishing fixed prices online.

Soups Appetizers Omelettes Salads & sandwiches Devilled chicken, fish or prawns Sri Lankan rice & curry Fried rice, noodles & pasta Fried and grilled dishes Fresh juices Tea, coffee & beverages

No fixed prices are published online. Please enquire for current availability, group dining and non-resident reservations.

Meal Plans

Choose how
you would like to dine.

Flexible meal plans to suit every traveller — whether you are here for one night, arriving with a group, or planning a longer stay. Rates are confirmed directly.

Available Plans
Room Only
Book just the room. A clean, comfortable stay with no meal obligation. Breakfast can be added the next morning on request.
Room with Breakfast
Your room includes breakfast each morning. Choose Option A or Option B — just let us know your preference at check-in.
Add-on Breakfast
Booked Room Only but changed your mind? Simply request breakfast the evening before — we will arrange it for the next morning.
